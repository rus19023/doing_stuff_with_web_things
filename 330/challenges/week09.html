Challenge 2 Proposal


The Problem

Many people who want to study the gospel of Jesus Christ don't have easy access to tool that enable them to learn easily. Some of these people are LDS, and they remember the Scripture Chases in Seminary, but there is no such thing once they out of Seminary.


The Solution


Scripture chase, in digital form!

App Title: Scripture Chase? LDS Doctrine Mastery?

App Description:

Using quiz style or flashcards that flip, you can study and memorize the scriptures that teach basic gospel doctrine, just like it's done in LDS Seminary classes.

App Design:

Scripture data comes from API/external JSON source.
